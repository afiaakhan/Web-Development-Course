Recipe # 04

Fettucine Chicken Paste

A rich and creamy Fettucine Chicken Paste recipe, made with tender chicken, flavorful garlic, and a hint of nutmeg. Serve with steaming hot fettucine noodles for a comforting meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fettucine noodles, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the chicken and garlic, and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Gradually add the chicken broth, whisking continuously, and bring to a simmer.
  5. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream, thyme, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  8. Serve the chicken paste over steaming hot fettucine noodles and enjoy!
